\velkynadpis{Rotation}

You will receive a number $n$ ($1\leq n \leq 20$) and a square of numbers of size $n\times n$ on the input. Your task is to rotate the square 90 degrees clockwise.

\nadpis{Examples}
\vstup
3
2 1 3 
3 2 5 
7 1 2 
\vystup
3 5 2
1 2 1
2 3 7
\koniec
